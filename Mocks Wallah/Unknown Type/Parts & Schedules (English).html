Which of the following subjects comes under the State List of the Indian Constitution?
    Port
    Railways
    Agriculture    
    Banking
Solution
The subjects of the Center and the State have been divided into three lists, Union List, State List and Concurrent List, under the Seventh Schedule of the Indian Constitution.
• The Union List deals with federal subjects like banking, currency, atomic energy, foreign affairs, defence, railways, post office, telegraph, income tax, custom duties etc. Presently there are 100 subjects [originally 97].
• The State List includes state subjects like agriculture, inter-state trade, police, fisheries, forests, local governments, theatre industry etc. Presently there are 61 subjects [originally 66].
• Concurrent List have related to issues common to the Center and the States both. Subjects included include stamp duty, drugs and poisons, electricity, newspapers, criminal law, labour welfare, factories, etc. Currently, a total of 52 subjects are included [originally 47].
