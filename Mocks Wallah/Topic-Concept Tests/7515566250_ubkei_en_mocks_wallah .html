Which monument was built in 1924 to welcome King George V and Queen Mary?
    Bombay Castle
    Gateway of India
    Victoria Memorial    
    Chattri Brighton
Solution
The Gateway of India is an arch-monument built in the early 20th century in the city of Mumbai, India. It was erected to commemorate the landing of King George V and Queen Mary, the first British Monarch to visit India. Its construction started on 31 March, 1913 and completed on 1924. The monument is built of yellow basalt and reinforced concrete. The final design of the monument was designed by Architect George Wittet.
