In which year did the Election Commission of India become a multi-member body for the first time in its history?
    2004
    1995
    2014    
    1989
Solution
The Election Commission was a single member body when it was set up in 1950 till October 1989, with only the Chief Election Commissioner as its only member. After the Election Commissioner Amendment Act 1989, it has been made a multimember body. Since October 1989 up to today, it is a three-member commission with one Chief Election Commissioner and two election commissioners.
