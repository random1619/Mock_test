Sri Akal Takht Sahib is located within the _________ complex.

    Gurudwara Bangla Sahib

    Patna Sahib

    Golden Temple    

    Paonta Sahib

Solution

The Akal Takht is one of five takhts [which means “seats of power”] of the Sikhs. It is located in the Golden Temple complex in Amritsar, Punjab, India.

The three Takhts are in Punjab:- Akal Takht in Amritsar, Sri Keshgarh Sahib in Anandpur and Sri Damdama Sahib at Talwandi Sabo Bhatinda district.

The other two of the five Takhts are:- Takht Sri Patna Sahib at Patna in Bihar and Takht Sri Hazoor Sahib at Nanded in Maharashtra.

Who built the Hawa Mahal situated in Jaipur?
    Rawal Jaisal
    Sawai Pratap Singh
    Chitrangad Mori    
    Rawal Ratan Singh
Solution
Hawa Mahal is located in Jaipur, the capital city of Rajasthan. Jaipur's signature building, the Hawa Mahal [Palace of Winds], a multi layered palace, was built by Kachhwaha Rajput Ruler Sawai Pratap Singh [grandson of Sawai Jai Singh who was the founder of Jaipur and son of Sawai Madhoo Singh] in 1799 A.D. The five storied structure was designed by Lal Chand Ustad. Red and Pink Sandstone have been used to build the Hawa Mahal. Twenty five percent of the land revenue collected by Marathas in the Deccan was called _______.

    Chauth

    Bhoga

    Byaga    

    Sardeshmukhi

Solution
  • Chauth’s Sanskrit meaning was one-fourth. Chauth was an annual tax nominally levied at 25 percent on revenue or produce. It was a regular tax or tribute imposed by the Maratha Empire in the Indian subcontinent.
  • It was imposed from the early 18th century.
  • It was levied on the lands which were under the nominal Mughal rule.
  • The “Sardeshmukhi” was an additional 10 percent levy on top of the Chauth. It was started by Koli Maharaja Som Shah of Ramnagar and It is a tribute to the king.
